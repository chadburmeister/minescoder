What we built

BDR Forecast

A weekly pipeline-forecasting app for a CRM & data-management company — where every BDR submits their PipeGen forecast and leaders track it against target in real time.

The brief

A growing BDR org was running its weekly forecast out of spreadsheets. They needed one place for every rep to submit S2 opps and pipeline — and for leaders to see the rollup instantly.

What we built

A full forecasting app: per-BDR weekly submissions, automatic team rollups vs. target, attainment and ramping flags, submission tracking, week-over-week history, and an admin panel — behind secure, role-based login.

Spreadsheets replaced by a live dashboard — forecasted pipeline vs. target, per-team progress, and submission status all visible at a glance, every week.
