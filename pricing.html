Questions

The fine print, in plain English

How can it be this much cheaper than an agency?

Because we build AI-native from day one. Modern AI tooling compresses months of routine engineering into days — and we pass that speed straight to you instead of billing it as hours. You get the same shipped product, faster, for a fraction of the cost.

Is the price really fixed?

Yes. We scope your build in writing — features, timeline, and price — before any work starts. As long as the scope doesn't change, the price doesn't either. Want to add something mid-build? We'll quote it as a small, clear add-on.

What if my idea doesn't fit a package?

Most don't fit perfectly — that's what the Scale tier and a quick call are for. Tell us what you're building and we'll map it to a fixed price and a timeline, usually within a day.

Do I own the code?

Completely. Clean, documented code in your repo, yours to keep, extend, or hand to another team. No lock-in, ever.

How does "pause anytime" work on the subscription?

Bill in months. If you hit a quiet stretch, pause your subscription and billing stops — your unused days roll over and your spot stays reserved for when you're ready to pick back up.

What about AI and hosting costs?

Those go directly to you at cost — hosting, AI API usage, and domains typically run $20–$500/mo depending on usage. No markup, no middleman. We'll help you set them up and keep them lean.

Tell us what you're building.

Send a few lines about your idea and you'll get a fixed price and a timeline back — usually within a day.
